Parking
For Local Commuters

The nearest parking structure is the North Wilson Parking Structure at 341 S Wilson Ave — $9/day or $27/week, reimbursable by AIM. Free street parking is available on California Blvd and S Wilson Ave, but is typically full by 8 am.
